about mussut

We bring your needs closer to you!

During pandemic, we saw struggles local businesses were going through while online systems were thriving. While our local businesses had all consumer needs, there was a lack of standard point. And this is how the idea of Mussut was born.


Our Goal Is To Re-Imagine The Way That The Common Man Shops And Act As A Bridge Between The Customer And The Market, Making The Process Smooth For Both Parties. We aim to make Indian Local markets empowered and give a seamless transition in the era of digitalization.

FAQ's

How can I use Mussut?

Mussut can currently be used via mobile application. We are working on bringing a web version to use.

What all can I order from Mussut?

You can order A-Z products from a great variety of products available on Mussut. We deal in Electronics, Accessories, Fitness, Grocery, Home Furnishing and more.

Can I order from a particular shop as well on Mussut?

Yes, you can easily search for a shop name and go through their listed products and buy from them easily.

Do I have to pay extra for same day delivery?

Not at all, Mussut does all the deliveries the same day the orders are placed. It is an exception if you order at night.You pay us a nominal amount for delivery.

Can I return/ exchange my order on Mussut?

If the product is eligible for return/ exchange, your product gets return exchanged hassle-free.

Can I order intercity from Mussut?

Mussut only focuses on hyper-local markets and currently you cannot order inter-city from Mussut.
