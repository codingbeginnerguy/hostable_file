FAQ's

How do I become a Mussut seller?

To become a Mussut seller, you have to fill the onboarding form, our team will contact you shortly for further process.

What is eligibility to be a Mussut seller?

If you are a registered business having a GST number for your shop, you are eligible to be a Mussut. Also, you need a static location of your business, which will be inspected by the Mussut team.

What are the commissions?

1. Mussut charges a nominal commission upto 5% of your listed price on the products you sell.
2. We charge no fee on delivery of products.
3. We also charge a reasonable monthly commission fee from you.

What are the benefits of joining Mussut early-bird offer?

1. Discounted commission for the first three months.
2. No monthly subscription for the first three month.
3. Personalized onboarding.
4. Give direct feedback.
5. One time minimal registration fee.

What do you charge for delivery ?

We charge nothing with sellers as of now. All our costs are covered above briefly.

How is Logistics handled by Mussut?

Mussut handles your logistics with a personal delivery crew. All your return exchanges are handled by us as well. We deliver to your customers the same day.

How does a customer access my shop?

They access your shop via Mussut app for users. Even new customers can access your listed products without specifically searching for your shop.

When do I get my earnings from Mussut?

You get your earnings from Mussut within 3 working days.
