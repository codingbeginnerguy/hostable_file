Return, Cancellation and Refund Policy

Last Updated: 18 February, 2022


1. Return and Exchanges

All products purchased on Mussut app can be returned/exchanged within the given timeline specified by Shopkeeper from the delivery date. The timeline as well as eligibility for return/exchange for the product is specified on the product detail page. To ensure full credit, your item(s) must be returned in new and unused condition, in the original boxes, and with all paperwork (including warranties cards, invoices, and so on). "New and unused" means the item has no scratches, blemishes, or blemishes, and it hasn't been sized or altered in any way.
Orders placed beyond 5 days are not eligible for refunds, but can be either replaced or fixed under the terms and conditions of the warranty of that product if the product is eligible for either of them.
In order to claim a refund for your order product contact us by filling the customer service form, you will be shortly contacted after you fill your queries.
After receiving the product our team as well as the seller will verify, approve the product and process the refund.

3. Refund Methods

  • We will inspect the product once it reaches us.
  • Following the inspection, we will notify you of the status of your refund.
  • Following approval, we will initiate a refund to your original payment method.
  • The total price of the product will be returned to the same mode of payment if the payment method used at the time of purchase was credit card, debit card, wallet, or net banking.
  • In the case of COD, you will provide an online method to us for your refund.
  • We will issue a refund to the original payment method within 4 working days (except COD).
  • If you return a partial product or one that has been tampered with, your refund will be denied. Instead, your order will be returned to you.
  • You must return the product, as well as all accessories and items that came with it.
